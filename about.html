About Delicious Eats

Our story, our flavors, our passion

Our Story

Delicious Eats began in 1995 when grandmother Maria brought her treasured recipe book from Italy to Brooklyn, New York. Starting with just four tables in a cozy corner storefront, she and her daughter Sofia began serving the same hearty dishes that had been passed down through three generations of their family.

Our signature dish, the "Nonna's Secret Lasagna," still follows Maria's original recipe, layered with her special blend of three cheeses and slow-cooked ragu that simmers for six hours. Each morning, our chefs make fresh pasta by hand, just as Maria did, keeping her tradition of excellence alive.

Over the years, we've grown from that tiny corner spot to become a neighborhood institution, but our commitment to authentic, home-style cooking hasn't changed. Sofia's son Marco now leads our kitchen, adding his own creative touch while honoring his grandmother's legacy. His modern interpretations, like the popular Truffle Mushroom Risotto and Sea Bass al Limone, have earned us recognition in the New York Times and Food & Wine magazine.

Today, we're proud to source many of our ingredients from local farms, supporting our community while ensuring the freshest flavors possible. Our herb garden, started by Maria herself, still provides the aromatic basil and oregano that give our dishes their distinctive taste.
